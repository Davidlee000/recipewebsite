Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 1 cup or 120 grams + extra for rolling the dough
Eggs 1 whole egg and 1 yolk

Instructions

  1. Prepare work surface
  2. Measure out flour
  3. Add flour to work surface
  4. Create a well in the middle of the flour
  5. Add eggs
  6. Mix the eggs
  7. Slowly incorporate the flour into the eggs
  8. If the dough is too wet add more flour
  9. Knead the dough until it bounces back when poked
  10. Let the dough rest for at least 10 minutes covered with plastic wrap
  11. Roll out the dough
  12. Fold the dough, sprinkling with flour so it doesn't stick to itself
  13. Cut the dough into strips
  14. Boil the pasta in salted water for 2-3 minutes
